Operator precedance in Javascript

    
Operator precedance refers to the priority given to operators while parsing a statement that has more than one operator performing operations
        in it Operators with higher priorities are resolved first but as goes down the list the priorty decreases and hence their resolution 

        The precedence of the multiple(*) and divide operator is the same but due to assiociativity from left to right the multiply operator
        will be resolved first as it comes first when we go from left to right .
